# ⚠️ Important: Goal Submission Workflow

## The Issue You're Seeing

Your **"ARR" goal** shows as **DRAFT** - this means it hasn't been submitted yet. 

**Draft goals CANNOT be approved by managers.** They must first be submitted by the employee.

---

## ✅ Correct Workflow to Approve the Goal

### Step 1: Switch to EMPLOYEE Role
1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. Look for an employee role option or use quick login

### Step 2: Navigate to "My Goals"
- Click **"My Goals"** in the sidebar (not Dashboard)
- You should see all your goals including the DRAFT ones

### Step 3: Submit for Approval
1. **Check weightage**: Total must equal **100%**
   - Your current goals: 20% + 10% + 20% + 25% + 25% = **100%** ✓
2. Click **"Submit for Approval"** button
3. All DRAFT goals become **SUBMITTED**

### Step 4: Switch to MANAGER Role
1. Go to **Dashboard** 
2. Switch to manager role (e.g., "Anita Desai")

### Step 5: Approve in Team Goals
1. Click **"Team Goals"** from sidebar
2. Look for **"Pending Approval"** section
3. You'll now see the ARR goal listed
4. Click ✓ button to **Approve**

---

## 📋 Goal Status Lifecycle

```
DRAFT          →    SUBMITTED       →    APPROVED
(Employee         (Employee ready    (Manager approved)
creating)         for review)

      ↙ (with comments)
  RETURNED 
(Manager asked
for changes)
```

---

## 🔑 Key Points

- **DRAFT goals** = Only for editing/deletion by employee, NOT visible to manager for approval
- **SUBMITTED goals** = Ready for manager review, can be approved or returned  
- **APPROVED goals** = Locked for achievement tracking, can't be edited
- **RETURNED goals** = Employee can edit them again and resubmit

If you don't see the submit button:

- Make sure you're on **"My Goals"** page (not Dashboard or other view)
